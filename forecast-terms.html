Forecast Terms

Here are some common terms used in weather forecasts:

  • Temperature: The measure of hotness or coldness measured in degrees Celsius or Fahrenheit.
  • Humidity: The amount of water vapor present in the air.
  • Wind Speed: The speed at which air is moving horizontally.
